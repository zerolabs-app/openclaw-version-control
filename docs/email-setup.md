# OpenClaw Email Setup

OpenClaw uses secure SMTP email delivery for task results and feedback.

For Gmail accounts, use a Gmail App Password instead of your normal Gmail password.

Google official instructions:

https://support.google.com/mail/answer/185833?hl=en

After creating your Gmail App Password:

1. Open OpenClaw Installer
2. Enter your Gmail address
3. Select Gmail as provider
4. Paste the Gmail App Password
5. Save Email Settings

Do NOT use your normal Gmail password.
